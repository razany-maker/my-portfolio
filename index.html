Currently, I'm a senior student at Jordan University of Science and Technology and I am passionate about entering the world of web development. I thrive on the challenge of bridging the gap between creative design and technical implementation. While I am just starting my journey, I am driven by a relentless curiosity to learn and a desire to craft user experiences that are not only visually appealing but also functionally robust.

My graduation project is my biggest work, where I wanted to create a tool that makes academic planning less stressful and more accessible for Computer Science students. This led to the development of the "CS Academic Advisor Chatbot". To bring this vision to life, I adopted—with the help of my team—a comprehensive design-to-development workflow. Utilizing Figma to prototype a user-centric interface focusing on clarity and ease of navigation for students, I then implemented these designs using responsive frontend code, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience. The platform features an Intelligent AI Chatbot that acts as a personal academic guide, Study Rooms that provide a campus-like environment for students to share knowledge and resources, and so much more.

Chatbot Interface

An AI-powered chatbot for the website offers real-time academic advising.

Profile Management Page

A comprehensive user dashboard allowing students to maintain their digital profile. Features include editable personal information fields, a read-only view of academic standing (Major, Year, GPA), and security settings for account protection.

Study Rooms

A virtual collaborative environment where students can connect beyond the classroom. The Study Rooms feature enables real-time knowledge sharing and resource exchange, bridging the gap between individual study and the interactive campus experience.

Faculty Information Page

A dedicated directory designed to bridge the gap between students and staff. This page allows students to easily discover and connect with faculty members. It features detailed profile cards displaying essential contact information, office hours, and research interests, making it simpler for students to find the right mentor or advisor for their academic needs.
